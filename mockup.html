Update Regarding the Columbia Bartending Agency and School of Mixology:

For the rest of 2015, the Columbia School of Mixology will remain closed as Columbia Entrepreneurship and friends of the CBA continue to re-imagine it as a truly student-run entrepreneurial endeavor. Anyone who is interested helping with the transition should reach out to Nathaniel Kelner (nhk2114@columbia.edu). The Columbia Bartending Agency will remain operational through the end of the year and continue to offer employment opportunities to all students who have been selected and trained as bartenders through previous School of Mixology terms. If you are a prospective or returning customer who is interested in booking bartenders, please visit: http://columbiabartending.com/hire-a-bartender.
